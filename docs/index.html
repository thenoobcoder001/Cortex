Everything in one window

🤖

Multi-Provider

Switch between Claude, Codex, Gemini CLI, Gemini API, and Groq without changing tools or workflows.

📁

Repo-Aware

AI runs in context of your actual files on disk. Reads, edits, and reasons about your real codebase — no copy-pasting.

⚡

Live Streaming

All providers stream token-by-token with stop-anytime support. See output as it arrives.

💾

Persistent History

Chat history saved per project and restored automatically on next launch. Never lose context.

🖥️

Built-in Terminal

Full PTY terminal wired directly into the app. Run commands and see results without leaving Cortex.

📱

Mobile Access

Cortex relay lets you drive your desktop AI from your phone. Same session, everywhere.
